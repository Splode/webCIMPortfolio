About Me

My name is Christopher Murphy and I’m passionate about crafting effective and engaging content in order to communicate the value of an idea, product, or experience. Curiosity and a love of learning have led me to both formal and informal education in a variety of design disciplines, ranging from fine woodworking to web design to architecture; I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts and a Masters of Architecture degree from the University of New Mexico.

In every design I seek to address a clearly identified problem with a fresh and elegant solution; taking diverse sets of project parameters and incorporating them into a thorough strategy for an engaging, user-friendly experience is my specialty. You can see a broader range of my work, including personal projects, at my personal portfolio site.
